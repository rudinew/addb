Mathematical model of road traffic accident development using the system analysis theory

Alexander Gusev, National Transport University, associate professor department of airports, https://orcid.org/0000-0002-0420-0443

Innesa Rutkovskaya, National Transport University, professor department of airports, https://orcid.org/0000-0001-7832-4222

Alla Gerasimenko, National Transport University, senior lecturer department of airports, https://orcid.org/0000-0001-7038-3703

 

Abstract: The article describes the principles and algorithms for creating a mathematical model of  the road traffic accidents on the highway section using system analysis theory and queuing system theory.

 

 Article language: Ukrainian
