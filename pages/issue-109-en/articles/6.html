A study of modern techniques of land management and cadastre for sustainable Cities.

Wen Mingming, Dean of the Human resource management Department,

Olena Slavinsʹka, National Transport University, Professor of the department of Transport construction and property management, https://orcid.org/0000-0002-9709-0078

Konstantin Mamonov, O. M. Beketov National University of Urban Economy in Kharkiv, professor of the department of land administration and GIS, https://orcid.org/0000-0002-7805-3132

 

Abstract: The work is devoted to the study of land resources of Ukraine and search of ways of improvement of modern approaches to land management decisions. Special attention is paid to determination of methods of rational use of land Fund of Ukraine. Investigated the processes occurring in the land sector of the country that require the development of new conceptual approaches to the implementation of the land administration processes on the basis of conscious analysis of modern problems of land management and cadaster.

 

Article language: English
