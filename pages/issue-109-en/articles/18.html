Abstract: We continue to publish proposals for improving transport systems. This article defines the 4th part of a large topic on the creation and adjustment of terms and definitions required for communication processes of communication, primarily researchers in this field of subject activity "researcher of transport systems (theorist) - researcher of transport systems (practitioner)". The second aspect of verвal interaction is to establish a communication relationship between "man - computer", which is important in modern conditions.
