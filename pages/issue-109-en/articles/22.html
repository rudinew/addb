Risks assessment on business planning

Ihor Kozarchuk. National Transport University, Associate Professor of the Department of Transport Construction and Property Management, https://orcid.org/0000-0003-4972-6016

 

Abstract: The article studies issues related to risks in entrepreneurial activity. The factors and reasons for its occurrence are analyzed. Methods for risk assessment are reviewed, among which special attention is paid to methods based on mathematical statistics and expert assessments. It is concluded that risk has a positive effect – it stimulates enterprises to improve the business planning system. 

Article language: English
