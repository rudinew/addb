New EU requirements for road infrastructure development

Alla Novikova, Chief of Research Center  Complex Transport Problems, State Enterprise “State Road Transport Research Institute”, https://orcid.org/0000-0002-7165-8673

Olga Sabanska, National Transport University post graduate student, Head of European integration division ASMAP Ukraine,  https://orcid.org/0000-0003-0593-5959

Alla Gerasimenko, National Transport University, Senior Lecturer, Airports Department, https://orcid.org/0000-0001-7038-3703

 

Abstract: The current situation is considered on the development of safe and secure parking areas for trucks in Europe.

The reports of the International Road Transport Union were analyzed on the European Union-funded project on Safe and secure truck parking areas for trucks in Europe and a final report on the study of safe and secure places for truck parking in the EU.

The analysis of European regulations is carried out on road infrastructure safety management, specification of truck parking zones, rules of working time and rest of the driver, as well as Ukrainian legislation on these issues.

Recommendations for possible measures to improve the legislation of Ukraine are provided in terms of establishing the SSTPA in view of the EU-Ukraine Association Agreement.
