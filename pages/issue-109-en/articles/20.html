Computer modeling of the process of soil sealing under the pipeline

Mykola Kuzminets, National Transport University, Head of the Department of Computer, Engineering Graphics and Design, https://orcid.org/0000-0002-9636-919X

 

Abstract: In the article, using computer simulation in Ansys environment, the working process of replacement equipment of a single-bucket excavator for soil compaction under the pipeline is investigated and the influence of working equipment parameters on the qualitative picture of soil compaction process and change of stress-strain state of main pipeline and soil is evaluated.
